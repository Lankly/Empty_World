Story
-----

  The world ended almost one hundred years ago. The Gods, which were worshipped so dearly, turned on Humanity. Kruzup and Rigalia, the Gods of Death, turned on the other Gods and sealed them away forever. They cleansed the surface of the Earth in Cosmic Fire,

  This is in this world in which you were born. But you have sworn to change this world and restore it. To this end, you have scoured the barren surface of the world for most of your life. You have finally come across a dungeon that you believe houses the vessel that the Gods are trapped in. Free them!
